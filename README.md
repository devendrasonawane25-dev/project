# Virtual Lab - Fourier Series

## 📚 About
A Virtual Lab experiment on **Fourier Series** developed for Engineering Mathematics course at **Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE)**.

## 🚀 Live Demo
[View Live Website](https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/virtual-lab-fourier-series/)

## ✨ Features
- **Aim & Theory**: Learn Fourier Series fundamentals
- **Pretest**: Assess your prior knowledge
- **Interactive Simulation**: 
  - Input custom functions, limits, and harmonics
  - Step-by-step calculation of Fourier coefficients (A₀, Aₙ, Bₙ)
  - Complete harmonic series equation generation
- **Posttest**: Evaluate your learning
- **References**: Further reading materials
- **Feedback System**: Share your experience

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- JavaScript (Vanilla)
