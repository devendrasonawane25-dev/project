A Virtual Lab on Fourier Series

Engineering Mathematics Experiment

Aim

The objective of this virtual lab experiment is to:

  • Understand the fundamentals of Fourier Series expansion
  • Learn to determine Fourier coefficients for periodic functions
  • Visualize the convergence of Fourier Series approximations
  • Analyze the behavior of Fourier Series for different types of functions
  • Apply Fourier Series concepts to solve engineering problems
